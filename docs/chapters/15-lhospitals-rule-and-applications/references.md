# References: L'Hospital's Rule and Contextual Applications

1. [L'Hôpital's rule](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/L%27H%C3%B4pital%27s_rule) - Wikipedia - Statement, proof, and examples of L'Hôpital's rule for 0/0 and infinity/infinity indeterminate forms. Core reference for the primary technique taught in this chapter.

2. [Indeterminate form](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Indeterminate_form) - Wikipedia - Classifies all seven indeterminate forms and explains transformation techniques. Essential for understanding which limits require L'Hôpital's rule or algebraic manipulation.

3. [Guillaume de l'Hôpital](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Guillaume_de_l%27H%C3%B4pital) - Wikipedia - Historical context of L'Hôpital and Johann Bernoulli's contributions. Adds mathematical history perspective to the chapter's narrative.

4. Calculus: Early Transcendentals (9th Edition) - James Stewart - Cengage Learning - Section 4.4 covers L'Hôpital's rule with careful attention to verifying hypotheses and handling all indeterminate form types.

5. Thomas' Calculus (15th Edition) - Joel Hass, Christopher Heil, Maurice Weir - Pearson - Section 4.5 presents L'Hôpital's rule with extensive examples and warnings about common misapplications.

6. [L'Hôpital's Rule](https://tutorial.math.lamar.edu/Classes/CalcI/LHospitalsRule.aspx) - Paul's Online Math Notes - Comprehensive examples covering 0/0, infinity/infinity, and converted forms (0·infinity, 1^infinity, etc.) with step-by-step solutions.

7. [L'Hôpital's Rule Practice](https://www.khanacademy.org/math/ap-calculus-ab/ab-diff-contextual-applications-new/ab-4-7) - Khan Academy - Interactive practice problems on applying L'Hôpital's rule with AP-aligned difficulty levels.

8. [L'Hôpital's Rule](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Gh48aOvWcxw) - Organic Chemistry Tutor - Video tutorial covering all indeterminate forms with clear explanations of when and how to apply the rule.

9. [Derivatives in Context](https://www.khanacademy.org/math/ap-calculus-ab/ab-diff-contextual-applications-new/ab-4-2) - Khan Academy - Practice problems interpreting the derivative in real-world contexts including rates, marginal analysis, and related rates.

10. [Indeterminate Forms and L'Hôpital's Rule](https://www.whitman.edu/mathematics/calculus_online/section04.10.html) - Whitman College Calculus - Open-source treatment with proofs, counterexamples showing when the rule fails, and exercises.
