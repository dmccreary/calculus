# References: Continuity

1. [Continuous function](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Continuous_function) - Wikipedia - Comprehensive coverage of continuity definitions, types of discontinuities, and properties of continuous functions. Core reference for all continuity concepts in this chapter.

2. [Classification of discontinuities](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Classification_of_discontinuities) - Wikipedia - Detailed taxonomy of removable, jump, and infinite discontinuities with visual examples. Directly supports the chapter's discontinuity classification activities.

3. [Intermediate value theorem](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Intermediate_value_theorem) - Wikipedia - Statement, proof, and applications of the IVT including root-finding. Provides rigorous foundation for the theorem introduced in this chapter.

4. Calculus: Early Transcendentals (9th Edition) - James Stewart - Cengage Learning - Section 2.5 covers continuity at a point, on an interval, and the IVT with clear diagrams showing each type of discontinuity.

5. Thomas' Calculus (15th Edition) - Joel Hass, Christopher Heil, Maurice Weir - Pearson - Section 2.5 presents the three-part continuity test with practical examples and thorough treatment of piecewise continuity.

6. [Continuity](https://tutorial.math.lamar.edu/Classes/CalcI/Continuity.aspx) - Paul's Online Math Notes - Step-by-step examples applying the three continuity conditions to various function types, with practice problems and solutions.

7. [Continuity at a Point](https://www.khanacademy.org/math/ap-calculus-ab/ab-limits-new/ab-1-11) - Khan Academy - Interactive lessons and practice on testing continuity conditions, identifying discontinuities, and applying the IVT.

8. [Types of Discontinuities](https://www.mathsisfun.com/calculus/continuity.html) - Math is Fun - Visual guide to removable, jump, and infinite discontinuities with interactive graphs that complement the chapter's MicroSims.

9. [Intermediate Value Theorem Explained](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9wEHflBNKwI) - Professor Leonard - Thorough lecture on the IVT with geometric intuition and worked examples showing how to verify theorem conditions.

10. [Continuity and the IVT](https://www.whitman.edu/mathematics/calculus_online/section02.05.html) - Whitman College Calculus - Open-source treatment with formal definitions, examples, and exercises covering continuity on closed intervals.
