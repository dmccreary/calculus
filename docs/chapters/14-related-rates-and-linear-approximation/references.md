# References: Related Rates and Linear Approximation

1. [Related rates](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Related_rates) - Wikipedia - Overview of the related rates technique with classic examples including ladder, balloon, and shadow problems. Core reference for the first half of this chapter.

2. [Linear approximation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Linear_approximation) - Wikipedia - The tangent line as a local linear approximation, error analysis, and relationship to differentials. Foundational reference for the linearization techniques in this chapter.

3. [Differential (mathematics)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Differential_(mathematics)) - Wikipedia - Formal treatment of differentials dx and dy and their use in approximation. Supports the chapter's discussion of differentials and error estimation.

4. Calculus: Early Transcendentals (9th Edition) - James Stewart - Cengage Learning - Sections 3.9 (related rates) and 3.10 (linear approximations and differentials) provide structured problem-solving strategies with diverse applications.

5. Thomas' Calculus (15th Edition) - Joel Hass, Christopher Heil, Maurice Weir - Pearson - Sections 3.10-3.11 cover related rates and linearization with excellent diagrams for geometric related rates problems.

6. [Related Rates](https://tutorial.math.lamar.edu/Classes/CalcI/RelatedRates.aspx) - Paul's Online Math Notes - Step-by-step related rates strategy with worked examples including cones, ladders, and expanding circles.

7. [Related Rates Problems](https://www.khanacademy.org/math/ap-calculus-ab/ab-diff-contextual-applications-new/ab-4-4) - Khan Academy - Interactive practice problems on related rates with video solutions covering the AP Calculus required problem types.

8. [Linear Approximation and Differentials](https://tutorial.math.lamar.edu/Classes/CalcI/LinearApproximations.aspx) - Paul's Online Math Notes - Clear examples of tangent line approximation with error analysis and differential notation.

9. [Related Rates](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=I9mVUOt1gvE) - Professor Leonard - Thorough lecture walking through the related rates problem-solving framework with multiple classic examples.

10. [Linearization and Differentials](https://www.whitman.edu/mathematics/calculus_online/section04.06.html) - Whitman College Calculus - Open-source section covering local linearity and differential approximation with proofs and exercises.
