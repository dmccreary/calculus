# References: Asymptotes and End Behavior

1. [Asymptote](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Asymptote) - Wikipedia - Covers vertical, horizontal, and oblique asymptotes with definitions, detection methods, and examples. Core reference for all asymptote concepts in this chapter.

2. [Rational function](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rational_function) - Wikipedia - Discusses domains, asymptotic behavior, and graphing of rational functions. Provides essential context for understanding where and why asymptotes occur.

3. [Limit at infinity](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Limit_of_a_function#Limits_at_infinity) - Wikipedia - Formal treatment of limits as x approaches infinity including dominant term analysis. Directly supports the end behavior analysis techniques in this chapter.

4. Calculus: Early Transcendentals (9th Edition) - James Stewart - Cengage Learning - Section 2.6 covers limits at infinity and horizontal asymptotes; Section 4.5 addresses asymptotic behavior in curve sketching.

5. Precalculus: Mathematics for Calculus (7th Edition) - James Stewart, Lothar Redlin, Saleem Watson - Cengage Learning - Chapter 3.6 provides thorough coverage of rational function graphing including all asymptote types.

6. [Infinite Limits and Vertical Asymptotes](https://tutorial.math.lamar.edu/Classes/CalcI/InfiniteLimits.aspx) - Paul's Online Math Notes - Clear examples of finding vertical asymptotes and evaluating infinite limits with sign analysis techniques.

7. [Limits at Infinity and Horizontal Asymptotes](https://tutorial.math.lamar.edu/Classes/CalcI/LimitsAtInfinityI.aspx) - Paul's Online Math Notes - Systematic approach to determining end behavior using dominant term comparison and leading coefficient ratios.

8. [End Behavior of Rational Functions](https://www.khanacademy.org/math/precalculus/x9e81a4f98389efdf:rational-functions/x9e81a4f98389efdf:end-behavior-of-rational-functions/a/end-behavior-of-rational-functions) - Khan Academy - Interactive lessons on how degree comparison determines horizontal and slant asymptotes in rational functions.

9. [Asymptotes](https://www.mathsisfun.com/algebra/asymptote.html) - Math is Fun - Visual guide to all three asymptote types with interactive examples, ideal for building geometric intuition alongside the chapter's MicroSims.

10. [Finding All Asymptotes of a Rational Function](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HeWOYm8R7dM) - Organic Chemistry Tutor - Step-by-step video tutorial on identifying vertical, horizontal, and oblique asymptotes with worked examples.
