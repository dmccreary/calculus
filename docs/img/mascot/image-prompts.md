# Delta the Slope-Walking Explorer — AI Image Generation Prompts

Use these prompts to generate the seven mascot images with a consistent style.
Recommended tools: DALL-E 3 (ChatGPT), Midjourney, or Adobe Firefly.
Target resolution: **512x512 px** or **1024x1024 px**, then resize to 200x200 px for web.
Format: **PNG with transparent background**.

---

## Base Character Description

Copy this base into every prompt below:

```
Please generate a new square png image with a transparent background.
Target resolution: **512x512 px**
Format: **PNG with transparent background** and no extra padding around the character pixels.

Style: modern 3D render with soft lighting, slightly cartoonish proportions,
clean lines, suitable for an educational textbook. Similar aesthetic to Pixar
or educational app mascots. On a transparent background, no text in image.
All parts of the body should be filled in with non-transparent pixel values.
Use a minimum of padding around the body.

A friendly triangular robot mascot named Delta, designed for a high school
calculus textbook. Delta has an equilateral triangle-shaped body made of
smooth teal/turquoise metal (hex #009688) with rounded corners for a friendly
appearance. The triangle is oriented with one vertex pointing upward like
the Greek letter delta (triangle).

Near the top vertex (apex), Delta has two large, expressive circular LED eyes
that glow warm orange (#FF5722), giving her a curious and enthusiastic
expression. At the two bottom vertices, small rubber-treaded wheels (dark
gray with visible treads) allow her to roll along mathematical curves.

Along the left edge of the triangle body, a glowing green LED strip (#00E676)
acts as a "slope indicator." Delta has small retractable measuring arms on
each side — thin metallic appendages with small grabber claws. At the very
top of the apex, a thin flexible antenna with a small glowing orange ball
at the tip wobbles playfully.

Next to Delta is a small backpack labeled "integral journal" where she stores
accumulated data.
```
